Learn practical SEO tips and tricks to boost your website ranking.

Keyword Research

Use tools like Google Keyword Planner, Ubersuggest, or Ahrefs to find relevant long-tail keywords that bring targeted traffic.

Meta Tag Optimization

Write concise and relevant meta titles and descriptions including your main keywords. Keep titles under 60 characters and descriptions under 160 characters.

Heading Structure

Use only one <h1> per page. Use <h2> and <h3> tags to organize your content semantically.

Image Optimization

Compress images and add descriptive alt attributes to improve page speed and accessibility.

Internal Linking

Link related articles and pages internally to help users navigate and improve SEO juice flow.
